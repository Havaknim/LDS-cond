Getting started

This page shows your shift details based on your link and line number. Your selections are saved automatically on this device, so you won't need to re-enter them each time you visit.

Using the calendar

Tap any date to see your shift details, including start time, finish time, turn number, and shift length. Use the arrows to move between months. A small yellow dot in the corner of a date indicates a payday. The Jump to section below the calendar lets you jump straight back to today, or skip forward to your next working day.

Permanent Earlies / Lates

If you're doing a permanent earlies or lates swap with another conductor, toggle this on and select whether you're doing earlies or lates. Then enter the two lines you're alternating between in either order and the calendar will automatically show the correct work for each week.

Annual Leave

When viewing a date in the shift details, press Mark week as Annual Leave to mark that whole week as A/L. The week will appear with a terracotta border on the calendar. Your annual leave is saved on this device only - it won't sync if you use a different device or browser, and clearing your browser history or cache will remove it.

Daily Leave

Use the Mark as Daily Leave button to record a daily leave request on a specific date. Enter your position in the queue (e.g. 12th STT) when prompted. Daily leave shows as a purple border on the calendar and is also saved on this device only.

Viewing diagrams

On any date with a real turn of duty, tap View diagram to see a simplified breakdown of your working - trains, breaks, reliefs and sign on/off times. Tap a headcode to open it on Realtime Trains.
